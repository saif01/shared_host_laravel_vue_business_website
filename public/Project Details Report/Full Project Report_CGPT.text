Business Website Platform - Feature Report

1) Project snapshot
- Configurable business website platform suitable for a wide range of industries.
- Unified admin console paired with a customer-facing site.
- Modular rollout so teams can start small and enable more capabilities over time.

2) Core value
- Centralized operations: dashboards, content, leads, announcements, and settings in one workspace.
- Flexible public pages: home, about, services, products, blog, careers, announcements, contact, and newsletter.
- Strong governance: roles, permissions, logging, and visibility controls across every module.

3) Admin workspace
- Dashboard and insights: live stat cards, charts, and AI-style alerts covering visitors, bot traffic, login health, new leads, and catalog activity; time-range filters (7/30/90/365 days).
- Lead and newsletter handling: intake tracking, status flows, assignments, unread indicators, notes, CSV export, and subscriber oversight with verification.
- Services management: full CRUD with descriptions, media, pricing bands, benefits, SEO fields, ordering, and read-only detail views to validate content.
- Products management: 11-tab workflow covering copy, media galleries with reorder, pricing options, categories/tags, specifications, features, downloads, FAQs, warranty details, SEO, publication controls, and file previews.
- Categories and tags: hierarchical structures with icons/images, ordering, publication toggles, slug automation, and usage tracking.
- Blog: authoring with featured images and excerpts, scheduling, multi-category/tagging, rich content, search, filters, sorting, and view counters.
- Careers: postings by department, location, and employment type with deadlines and ordering; applications include statuses, notes, resume upload/download, validation, and statistics.
- Announcements: types (news, offers, events, holidays, urgent alerts) and display modes (banner, popup, ticker, page section) with scheduling, priority, media/video/embed support, external links, and visibility controls.
- Users, roles, permissions: multi-role users, profile fields, avatar management; grouped permissions securing every feature area with protected system roles.
- Logs and audits: visitor and login logs with search, filters, time-series data, device/browser/OS breakdowns, bot detection, and bulk cleanup.
- Settings and branding: site identity, color choices, logos/favicons, SEO defaults, social links, email/SMTP, contact page content, and per-section toggles for the homepage.

4) Public website experience
- Homepage: configurable hero, stats, trusted logos, services, reasons to choose, testimonials, featured products, and CTA blocks, each with visibility toggles.
- Products: category filtering, search across copy/SKU/specs, sorting, featured badges, responsive grids, sticky filters, and a comparison tool; detail pages with galleries and zoom, key features, quick specs, tabbed content (overview, specs, features, downloads, FAQs, warranty), related items, and trust badges.
- Services: grid listing with detailed service pages highlighting benefits, media, and pricing ranges.
- Blog: searchable, filterable listing with category navigation and modern hero treatment; detail pages with overlays, breadcrumbs, author card, social sharing, tags/categories, related posts, and automatic view increments.
- Careers: public listings with filters, active-only checks, and deadline-aware applications with resume upload and validation.
- Announcements: automatic, prioritized popups/dialogs with media or video, next/skip options, remember-dismiss behavior, and responsive sizing for desktop and mobile.
- Contact and newsletter: lead-generating contact form plus footer subscription with verification to keep mailing lists clean.
- SEO and performance: per-page meta and OG coverage, structured data, sitemap generation, responsive design, lazy loading, and optimized assets for fast loads.

5) Insights and automation
- AI-style alerts highlight bot surges, login issues, urgent leads, and engagement peaks.
- Time filters across charts keep operators focused on the right window of activity.

6) Governance and safety
- Role-based access and granular permissions control every admin route and action.
- Bot detection, IP/device tracking, and structured logs support incident review and fraud reduction.
- Media handling with previews keeps assets organized across products, services, blogs, and announcements.

7) Module flexibility and extensibility
- Modules can be enabled on demand: services, products, portfolio, blog, FAQ, careers, announcements, booking, events, branches, with future e-commerce and multi-language.
- Designed for incremental growth: start with core pages, then layer in catalogs, recruiting, campaigns, or new locations without rework.

8) Operational readiness
- Seed data creates roles, demo users, module definitions, and sample content to speed onboarding.
- Straightforward setup steps (install, configure environment, migrate, seed, build assets) support rapid local or staged deployments.
